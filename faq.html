Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a trip?

You can go to the home page, select a destination, and click "Book Trip" to start booking.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept debit cards, credit cards, UPI, and net banking.

3. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your trip from your account. Cancellation charges may apply.

4. How will I get my ticket?

After booking, your ticket and trip details will be sent to your email.

5. Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is optional. You can add it during booking.
